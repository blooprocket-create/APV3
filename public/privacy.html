Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 3, 2025

Studio Nova respects your privacy. We collect the minimum amount of personal information required to deliver digital products, manage service engagements, and provide account access. This includes your name, email address, login credentials, and optional details you share while briefing service requests.

Passwords are securely hashed using bcrypt. Session cookies are HttpOnly and scoped to Studio Nova properties. We never sell or rent personal data and only share information with trusted vendors required to deliver the services described.

You can request data export or deletion at any time by emailing privacy@studionova.dev. We respond within 7 business days.
